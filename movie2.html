Stree(2018)

In the small town of Chanderi, the menfolk live in fear of an evil spirit named "Stree" who abducts men in the night. Based on the urban legend of "Nale Ba" that went viral in Karnataka in the 1990s. Stree was theatrically released worldwide on 31 August 2018 and received positive reviews from critics, becoming a major commercial success at the box-office.

Director: Amar Kaushik
Stars: Rajkummar Rao, Shraddha Kapoor
