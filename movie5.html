Meri Pyaari Bindu(2017)

Abhimanyu Roy returns to his roots to write an old-fashioned love story, 3 years in the making. This writer's block is called Bindu. Meri Pyaari Bindu was released worldwide on 12 May 2017 to mixed-to-positive reviews, with praise for its novel concept, and the lead pair's chemistry and performances, but criticism for its narrative and pacing.

Director: Akshay Roy
Stars: Ayushmann Khurrana, Parineeti Chopra
